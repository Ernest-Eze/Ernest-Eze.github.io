About Me

My name is Ernest Eze and I love telling stories with data and uncovering insights from them. I Started my career in the financial sector as an SME & Personal Banker. My pivoting to data 5 years ago can best be described as accidental even though those have been some of the most fulfilling periods of my career! I started my data analytics journey by using Excel and PowerPoint to build tables & reports and then stepped it up by learning via online resources at self-paced level. I have now gone further to complete a data analytics diploma with SAIT so I can have a well-rounded experience and knowledge. My journey into the field of data has made a tremendous impact on my life as well as others. My goal is to continue to share my journey with the rest of the world while contributing my quota in making the world a better place. My core competencies are data cleaning, modelling, ETL, reporting, visualization and building collaborations. My favorite (everyday tools) would include SQL, Excel, Big query, SSIS, Power-BI and Tableau. I am still working and improving on my python skills. My machine learning project was built using frameworks in Python such as Pandas, NumPy, Scikit Learn, NLTK and Visualized using Plotly and Matplotlib. When I am not analyzing datasets, I read about new trends in the data industry, play soccer, watch TV and relax with family. You are welcome to keep coming back here to see new content.
